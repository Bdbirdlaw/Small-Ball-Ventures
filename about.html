★ ABOUT THE FRANCHISE

We're general managers, not gamblers.

Small Ball Ventures is a Nashville-based venture studio that builds and capitalizes a portfolio of disciplined, JV-anchored startups designed for ~$25M strategic exits. We treat venture investing the way Billy Beane treated baseball: as a math problem with a misallocated market.

★ Founder First

Build the right team.

We want hungry founders and experienced operators rowing the boat together. The best teams in sports history were clubs made up of different players with different strengths and weaknesses. We believe an algorithmic approach to pairing veteran operators with hungry founders is a recipe for building great companies with consistent returns.

We're co-founding partners, not passive check-writers. We take one board seat (never the chair), put the in-house studio behind every company (legal, finance, product, design, go-to-market), and step back the moment the team can operate without us. If the founder wins, we win.

Meet the front office.

Operators, dealmakers, and industry insiders. We've built and sold companies, run P&Ls, and learned the hard way. Now we're building the team we wish we'd had.

#01

Joe Barba

★ MANAGING PARTNER

Joe Barba is the Managing Partner at Small Ball Ventures, where he helps identify, evaluate, and support high-potential companies with strong founder-market fit, scalable distribution, and clear paths to revenue growth. An operator by background, Joe brings firsthand experience across company formation, business development, strategic partnerships, and exit execution, including co-founding Vig It, which was later acquired by Savage Ventures.

At Small Ball Ventures, Joe applies a quantitative, investor-first lens to early-stage opportunities, focusing on market size, revenue velocity, customer acquisition efficiency, founder quality, and upside potential. His strength is separating real commercial momentum from noise and backing companies with the practical ingredients needed to compound: strong operators, efficient go-to-market motion, and a credible path to enterprise value creation.
