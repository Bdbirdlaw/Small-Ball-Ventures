★ ABOUT THE FRANCHISE

We're general managers, not gamblers.

Small Ball Ventures is a Nashville-based venture studio that builds and capitalizes a portfolio of disciplined, JV-anchored startups designed for ~$25M strategic exits. We treat venture investing the way Billy Beane treated baseball: as a math problem with a misallocated market.

★ Origin Story

Born from frustration with the Power Law.

We've worked inside top-quartile venture funds, family offices, and operator-led roll-ups. We watched dozens of perfectly viable companies (businesses on a clear path to a profitable $20M outcome) get pushed to "go bigger" until they fell off the cliff.

We built Small Ball to back the founders who would have been winners under the old playbook: capital-efficient, customer-obsessed, and allergic to hype.

★ Clubhouse Rules

Six things we believe.

★ 01

OBP > Slugging.

Reliable singles compound into a championship season. Power hitters strike out three times as often.

★ 02

The JV is the moat.

If we can't sign a partner before incorporation, the company doesn't exist. Period.

★ 03

Founders own the field.

We coach from the dugout. Founders run the bases, set the lineup, and call the plays.

★ 04

The exit is engineered.

Liquidity is a feature designed in at the cap table, not a hope dropped on the bankers.

★ 05

Capital is the bat, not the swing.

Money doesn't build companies. People do. We invest the cash, then we shut up and assist.

★ 06

Stats over stories.

If a deck depends on adjectives, it's not ready. Bring us a spreadsheet and a customer.
