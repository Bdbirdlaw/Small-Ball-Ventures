★ ABOUT THE FRANCHISE

We're general managers, not gamblers.

Small Ball Ventures is a Nashville-based venture studio that builds and capitalizes a portfolio of disciplined, JV-anchored startups designed for ~$25M strategic exits. We treat venture investing the way Billy Beane treated baseball: as a math problem with a misallocated market.

★ Founder First

Build the right team.

We pair hungry founders with experienced operators who have already built, scaled, and exited companies. The best teams are not built from identical players. They are built from complementary strengths. At Small Ball Ventures, we use a systematic, data-informed approach to match founder ambition with operator experience, creating a repeatable model for building stronger companies and more consistent investor outcomes.

We're co-founding partners, not passive check-writers. We take one board seat (never the chair), put the in-house studio behind every company (legal, finance, product, design, go-to-market), and step back the moment the team can operate without us. If the founder wins, we win.

Meet the front office.

Operators, dealmakers, and industry insiders. We've built and sold companies, run P&Ls, and learned the hard way. Now we're building the team we wish we'd had.

★ Managing Partner
#01

Joe Barba

★ MANAGING PARTNER

Joe Barba is the Managing Partner at Small Ball Ventures, where he helps identify, evaluate, and support high-potential companies with strong founder-market fit, scalable distribution, and clear paths to revenue growth. An operator by background, Joe brings firsthand experience across company formation, business development, strategic partnerships, and exit execution, including co-founding Vig It, which was later acquired by Savage Ventures.

At Small Ball Ventures, Joe applies a quantitative, investor-first lens to early-stage opportunities, focusing on market size, revenue velocity, customer acquisition efficiency, founder quality, and upside potential. His strength is separating real commercial momentum from noise and backing companies with the practical ingredients needed to compound: strong operators, efficient go-to-market motion, and a credible path to enterprise value creation.

★ Operations
#02

Noah Zak

★ ENTREPRENEUR IN RESIDENCE

Noah Zak earned both his Bachelor's degree in Business and MBA from Missouri State University. While pursuing his MBA, he founded TheSportsDemand, a sports-focused media company that reached over 50 million Gen-Z users across digital platforms.

He previously served as Director of Marketing and Development at Savage Sports, the sports and entertainment division of Nashville-based venture capital firm Savage Ventures. In this role, he co-led a creative team that secured $14 million in partnership revenue from major brands including DraftKings, FanDuel, and AB InBev. Noah has since specialized in lead generation, overseeing the technical execution of campaigns that have generated hundreds of thousands of leads across law, home services, and insurance sectors.

#03

Bobby D'Angelo

★ ENTREPRENEUR IN RESIDENCE

Bobby D'Angelo joined Total Frat Move during the COVID-19 pandemic to revitalize their digital presence. Eight months later, Savage Ventures acquired the company and brought Bobby on to lead content strategy. During his tenure at Savage, he built social media audiences from inception to over 100,000 followers across multiple platforms, grew newsletter subscriptions to 75,000+, and co-led the creative team that generated $14 million in partnership revenue alongside Noah Zak.

Bobby subsequently joined the growth team at fintech company Invest with Roots, contributing to their expansion from 5,624 to over 14,000 investors. He has also directed creative strategy for lead generation campaigns across law, home services, and insurance, and more. He is currently the co-founder of AgBid.ai.
