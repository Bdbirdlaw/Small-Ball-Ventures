What gets a tryout.

We don't need a finished company. We need three things on the table — or at least one and a clear path to the other two.

★ 01 — FOUNDER

The Right Bat.

2nd or 3rd-time operator. Capital-efficient instincts. Allergic to vanity. Willing to share equity with a JV partner from Day One.

★ 02 — JV PARTNER

An incumbent, distributor, or operator with customers, regulatory cover, or distribution they'll commit to your company at incorporation.
